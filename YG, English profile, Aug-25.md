## **2. Professional Services** 

We provide a full range of core public accounting and financial services in the areas of auditing, accounting, taxes and business establishment to individuals, large and small businesses as well as not-for-profit organizations. 

## **2.1. Audit & Assurance Services** 

Shareholders, creditors, and other supervisory governmental authorities often need assurance that the financial statements fairly represent the financial position of an entity. 

An audit is a methodical review aiming to examine the financial statements, which requires a review of internal controls, testing of selected transactions, and communication with third parties. Based on our findings, we issue a report on whether the financial statements are fairly stated and free of material misstatements as well as its compliance with applied laws and regulations. 

## **2.2. Accounting & outsourcing Services** 

We aim to provide financial information to our clients in a timely and accurate manner because a well-organized financial records ensure that your business operations will run more efficiently on a daily basis. 

Our firm provides a full range of accounting services for business and not-for-profit organizations including bookkeeping, payroll related services, financial statements preparation, and set-up of accounting system for recently established entities.
